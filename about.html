About PawCare

We create reliable, loving care for your dog — with a focus on safety, enrichment, and honest communication.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, reliable dog care that brings peace of mind to pet parents and joy to dogs. We prioritize safety, enrichment, and individualized plans.

Our Vision

A community where every dog has access to professional care and meaningful social time — whether for daily walks, training, or overnight stays.

Our Promise

Clear communication, certified caregivers, GPS-tracked walks, and daily photo updates. We treat every dog as part of the family.
